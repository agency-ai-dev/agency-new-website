Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Agency AI, LLC ("Agency AI", "we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit www.agencyai.app or use our platform.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website when you visit it. They help the website remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and session state, making your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following categories of cookies:

Category Purpose Examples
Strictly Necessary Required for the site and app to function. Cannot be disabled. Session tokens, authentication state
Analytics Help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it. Google Analytics, page view tracking
Marketing Used to deliver relevant ads and track campaign performance across platforms. Meta Pixel, Google Ads conversion tags
Preferences Remember your settings and choices to personalize your experience. Language, region, UI preferences

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our site are set by third-party services we use, including:

  • Google Analytics — website traffic and behavior analysis
  • Meta Pixel — ad performance measurement and audience building
  • Google Ads — conversion tracking for paid campaigns
  • Shopify — app session management and billing

These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies. We encourage you to review them directly.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies from specific sites
  • Block all cookies entirely (note: this may break site functionality)

To opt out of Google Analytics tracking, you can use the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. To manage Meta ad preferences, visit Facebook Ad Settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
