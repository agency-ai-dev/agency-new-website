How to Set Up Google & YouTube with Shopify (2026) – Complete Guide

May 21, 2026 Agency AI Team 5 min read

Bringing your Shopify store to Google and YouTube is one of the fastest ways to reach high-intent shoppers. With the Google & YouTube app by Shopify, you can sync your product catalog, run ads, and track performance — all in one place. This tutorial walks you through setting it up, step by step.

Before You Start

Prerequisites
  • A Shopify store with products added and a primary domain set
  • A Google Account (Gmail)
  • A Google Merchant Center account
  • A Google Ads account (if you plan to run paid campaigns)
  • A YouTube channel connected to your Google account (for shopping on YouTube)

Tip: If you don't have a Google Merchant Center or Ads account yet, Shopify will help you create them during setup.

The Setup Steps

1

Install the Google & YouTube App

From your Shopify admin, go to Apps & sales channels and open the Shopify App Store. Search for Google & YouTube by Shopify, then click Install and Add app. This app is the hub for all your Google integrations — Shopping, Ads, and YouTube.

2

Connect Your Google Account

Open the Google & YouTube app and click Connect Google Account. Log in and grant Shopify permission to access:

  • Google Merchant Center
  • Google Ads
  • YouTube (optional but recommended)
3

Sync Products to Google Merchant Center

Select the product feed you want to submit (default: All Products), then choose your target country and language. Set your shipping settings (Shopify often syncs these automatically) and confirm your tax settings — especially for U.S. stores. Click Complete Setup to start syncing. Products are submitted to Google for review, which typically takes 1–3 days.

4

Enable Free Listings & Ads

Once your products are approved, enable Free Listings to show up in Google Shopping and Google Search at no cost. To go further, turn on a Performance Max campaign:

  • Smart bidding promotes products across Search, Shopping, Gmail, YouTube, and Display
  • Set a daily budget and choose your product feed
  • Google builds the ads automatically from your product data and assets
5

Connect Your YouTube Channel

If you want to sell on YouTube, confirm your channel is eligible for shopping features, then connect it inside the app. Choose the products to feature, approve the YouTube Shopping terms, and your products can appear below videos, in the channel store tab, and during livestreams.

Optimize Your Listings

To maximize reach and approval rates:

  • Use high-resolution product images
  • Write clear product titles and descriptions
  • Include accurate GTINs or MPNs — Google uses these to identify items
  • Monitor product status and fix any issues flagged in the app dashboard

Common Issues & Fixes

Products disapproved
Check for policy violations or missing information like images, GTINs, or shipping rates.
Ads not showing
Confirm your daily budget, feed approval status, and that the campaign is active.
Merchant Center errors
Use the Diagnostics tab inside your Merchant Center account to find and resolve flagged issues.

You're Ready to Sell on Google & YouTube

With everything connected, your Shopify products are now discoverable across some of the internet's most-used platforms:

  • Google Shopping (free + paid)
  • Google Search
  • YouTube
  • Gmail
  • Discover Feed
